## Notes
- No Lalaparuza Smackdown episode this season.
- COVID-19 halted in-person finale production. The **Grand Finale (Ep 14)** was pre-taped with each finalist filming from home. The tournament-bracket format of S10/S11 was abandoned; instead each of the top three performed a **solo lip sync** to a song she chose, followed by a group performance of "Survivor" by Destiny's Child. RuPaul judged the pre-recorded tapes and crowned Jaida Essence Hall. Heidi N Closet was named Miss Congeniality.
- Ep 1 and Ep 2 were split-premiere lip syncs with no elimination (the two top-placers of each half faced off to determine a winner of the week).
- Ep 10 ("Superfan Makeover") was the season's double shantay — Heidi and Jackie Cox both survived.
- Ep 8 Jan-vs-Widow and Ep 9 Jackie-vs-Widow are often cited as the season's most controversial/emotional eliminations.
- Sherry Pie's Ep 2 appearance was later disqualified (off-screen) before the finale due to catfishing scandal — she was not included in the finale but her in-season lip sync still aired.
