# All Stars 5 — Lip Syncs

Format: Lip Sync Assassins. Each week one all-star is crowned "Top All Star" after the main challenge; she lip-syncs against a pre-selected "Lip Sync Assassin" (a returning queen from a past season). The Top All Star reveals her own lipstick choice; the group has already voted on a bottom queen. If the Top All Star wins, her lipstick decides the elimination; if the Assassin wins, the group's vote decides. Ties invoke both. Finale is a four-way bracket.
