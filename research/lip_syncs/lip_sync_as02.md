# All Stars 2 — Lip Syncs

Format note: AS2 introduced Lip Sync for Your Legacy. The top two queens of the week faced off; the winner earned $10,000 and the power to eliminate one of the bottom queens (winner != eliminated). Episode 5 ("Revenge of the Queens") is a special case — both queens won and re-entered; both jointly eliminated Phi Phi. The finale was a three-way lip sync for the crown.
