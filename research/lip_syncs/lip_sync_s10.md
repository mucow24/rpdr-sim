## Notes
- No Lalaparuza Smackdown episode this season.
- Ep 14 used the tournament "Lip-Sync Smackdown for the Crown" format (first year of this format), with Eureka eliminated in the semifinal round against Aquaria (Kameron advanced past Asia, then faced Aquaria in the final).
- Ep 9 ("Breastworld") was the season's lone double-shantay — Eureka and Kameron both survived.
- Ep 12 featured a non-elimination final four group performance of "Call Me Mother" to set the finale bracket.
