# Season 18 — Lip Syncs

Season 18 aired on MTV from January 2, 2026 and concluded April 2026; as of 2026-04-21 the finale has aired and Myki Meeks was crowned the winner. The season reprised the Rate-a-Queen talent-show twist across episodes 1, 5, 6, and 10, where the top two lip synced for the win with no queen eliminated. Episodes 2–4, 6–9, and 11–14 were standard eliminations. Episode 15 was the returning-queens LaLaPaRuZa Smackdown. Episode 16 was the grand finale.
