## Notes
- No Lalaparuza Smackdown episode this season.
- Ep 3 was a six-way "smackdown-style" lip sync (not branded Lalaparuza) following a diva worship challenge; Honey Davenport was the sole elimination.
- The pre-season "Lip Sync Extravaganza Extravaganza" between Brooke Lynn and Yvie exists only as a WOW Presents Plus promotional special and is **non-canonical** — it did not determine any in-show outcome.
- The Ep 8 "Sorry Not Sorry" double shantay came after Brooke Lynn's Celine Dion flop in Snatch Game and is widely regarded as one of the greatest lip syncs in franchise history.
- Ep 14 used the tournament "Lip Sync Smackdown for the Crown" bracket: final four → final two → crowning. A'keria and Silky were eliminated in round 1; Yvie defeated Brooke Lynn in the final.
