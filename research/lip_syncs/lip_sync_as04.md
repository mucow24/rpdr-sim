# All Stars 4 — Lip Syncs

Format: Lip Sync for Your Legacy. Top two all-stars of the week lip-sync; the winner picks which bottom queen to eliminate using her lipstick. Episode 5 had no elimination (rules suspended). Episode 6 was the LaLaPaRUza smackdown — eliminated queens returned and competed to re-enter; four 1v1 lip syncs, loser sashayed, winner stayed/re-entered.
