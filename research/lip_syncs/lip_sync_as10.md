# All Stars 10 — Lip Syncs

Format: "Tournament of All Stars" — 18 queens split into three brackets of 6. Each bracket lasts 3 episodes; top 3 per bracket (by points) advance to a shared semi-finals, where the bottom two lip sync for their lives with an elimination. Finale is a Lip-Sync LaLaPaRuZa Smackdown for the Crown among the surviving 7 plus one lottery-returning queen (Kerri Colby). Regular-season lip syncs: Top 2 queens per episode earn 2 points each and lip sync to "Lip Sync for Your Legacy"; winner earns $10K and a bonus point. Season aired May–July 2025; complete and won by Ginger Minj (her first win on a fourth attempt).
