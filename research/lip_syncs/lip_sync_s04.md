# Season 4 — Lip Syncs

Source: Wikipedia "RuPaul's Drag Race season 4" (Lip syncs + Episodes sections), cross-checked against episode summaries on the same page.

Notes on format:
- S4 had no Lalaparuza Smackdown (format introduced in S15).
- Ep 8 lip sync is unique: Willam was disqualified immediately after lip-syncing (for receiving conjugal visits from his husband), so both Phi Phi and Sharon were saved.
- The finale (Ep 12, "The Fabulous Bitch Ball") was the first-ever three-way LSFYL. RuPaul did NOT crown on stage — three alternate endings were filmed; the actual crowning happened in Ep 13 "Reunited" in front of a live audience. There was no separate LSFYL at the reunion; Sharon was simply announced as the winner.
