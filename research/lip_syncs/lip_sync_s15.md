# Season 15 — Lip Syncs

Note on S15 format: Standard elimination format throughout. Episode 8 was the Lip Sync LaLaPaRuZa Smackdown (with the 10 eliminated queens returning — eventually producing a bracket that ended with Jax eliminated). Episode 1 was a non-elimination premiere (no lip sync). The finale (Ep 16) used a Top 4 solo original-song format: four finalists performed solo, then the chosen Top 2 lip synced for the crown.
