# Season 13 — Lip Syncs

Note on S13 format: The premiere (Ep 1) used the "Porkchop" split — six lip syncs among ALL the queens to sort them into Winners Circle vs. Porkchop Queens. Eps 2 and 3 were "Lip Sync for the Win" episodes — the top two queens of the week lip synced for a $5,000 cash tip (no elimination). Elimination lip syncs resumed in Episode 4. The chocolate bar / tipping the scales twist was NOT a season 13 mechanic (that's Season 14).
