# Season 17 — Lip Syncs

Season 17 introduced the "Badonka Dunk Tank" twist: the lip sync loser in certain early episodes could pull a lever to dunk Michelle Visage and save themselves from elimination. Episodes 1 and 2 top-two lip syncs were for the win (non-elim, maxi-challenge prize). Hormona Lisa (ep 2) and Arrietty (ep 5) lost their lip syncs but were both saved by the Badonka Dunk Tank. Episode 13 ended in a double shantay (no elimination). The finale used the original-song + top-2-lip-sync-for-the-crown format.
