# All Stars 3 — Lip Syncs

Format note: AS3 continued Lip Sync for Your Legacy. Episode 6 featured BenDeLaCreme's unprecedented self-elimination AND the return of a previously eliminated queen (Morgan McMichaels). The finale introduced the jury twist — eliminated queens voted on who advanced to the top two, and the top two lip synced for the crown. A top-4 group performance of "Kitty Girl" preceded the top-two duel.
