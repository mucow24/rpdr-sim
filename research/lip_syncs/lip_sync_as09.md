# All Stars 9 — Lip Syncs

Format: "All Winners Non-Elimination" / "Lip Sync for Your Charity" — eight queens compete for charity, no eliminations during the regular season. Top 2 queens per week earn Beautiful Benefactress Badges and lip sync for a $10K charity donation; winner may "cut off" one safe queen next week. Top 3 on badges advance to final lip sync for the crown.
