# Season 14 — Lip Syncs

Note on S14 format: Introduced the Chocolate Bar twist — the lip sync winner would then "eliminate" a queen of their choice (including potentially themselves if they held a golden chocolate bar). The Lip Sync LaLaPaRuZa Smackdown was Episode 11 ("An Extra Special Episode"), where 7 previously-bottomed queens ran a bracket to decide who stayed. The finale (Ep 16) used a Top 5 solo-song format, not a traditional bracket — each finalist performed a solo original song, then the picked top 2 lip synced for the crown.
