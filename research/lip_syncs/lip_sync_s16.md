# Season 16 — Lip Syncs

Season 16 opened with the "Rate-a-Queen" twist: the cast was split into two groups of seven, and episodes 1 and 2 were talent-show Rate-a-Queen rounds with no elimination. In those episodes the queens ranked each other and the top two of the week lip synced for the win (plus a $5,000 cash tip), not to stay. Regular eliminations started in episode 3. The finale used the "lip sync for the crown" format: top 3 performed original verses, Ru eliminated one, the remaining two lip synced for the crown.
