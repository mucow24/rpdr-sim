# All Stars 7 — Lip Syncs

Format: "All Winners" / "Legendary Legend Stars" — no eliminations during the regular season. Each week the top 2 queens earn a star and compete in a Lip Sync for Your Legacy; winner gets $10K tip and blocks a safe queen from earning a star the following week. Top 4 on stars advance to a Lip Sync LaLaPaRuZa Smackdown finale (Queen of All Queens); the bottom 4 compete in a parallel "Queen of She Done Already Done Had Herses" Smackdown for $50K.
