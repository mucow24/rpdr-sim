# All Stars 6 — Lip Syncs

Format note: AS6 kept the Lip Sync Assassins format from AS5 (Top All Star vs. surprise returning assassin), not a return to standard LSFYL. The twists layered on top were the "Game Within a Game" (hidden badges rewarded queens; revealed in ep 11) and the RuDemption Lip Sync Smackdown — a weekly bracket among eliminated queens, with the final winner re-entering. Finale was a four-way single-song lip sync.
