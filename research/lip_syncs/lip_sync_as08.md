# All Stars 8 — Lip Syncs

Format: "Lip Sync for Your Legacy" with a new twist — a Lip Sync Assassin (a guest queen from the franchise) enters each week and faces the Top All Star. Winner receives $10K tip. The eliminated queen is chosen either by the winning assassin (via the group's vote) or by the top all star (when they win the lip sync). Parallel "Fame Games" fan-vote competition among eliminated queens for $60K.
