# Season 5 — Lip Syncs

Source: Wikipedia "RuPaul's Drag Race season 5" (Lip syncs + Episodes sections), cross-checked against episode summaries.

Notes on format:
- S5 had no Lalaparuza Smackdown (format introduced in S15).
- Ep 4 ("Black Swan: Why It Gotta Be Black?") had a double sashay: after an underwhelming Oops!... I Did It Again, RuPaul eliminated BOTH Honey Mahogany and Vivienne Pinay — first-ever double sashay in franchise history.
- Ep 7 ("RuPaul Roast") is the famous Alyssa vs Roxxxy LSFYL with the Roxxxy wig-reveal — both queens declared winners, no one eliminated.
- Coco Montrese lip-synced 4 times, sending Monica, Jade, and Alyssa home (a season record at the time).
- Finale (Ep 13, "Countdown to the Crown"): Top 3 lip-synced together to "The Beginning" — no one eliminated, no winner crowned on stage.
- Ep 14 "Reunited" live finale: Jinkx announced as winner outright; no additional LSFYL was performed to determine the crown.
